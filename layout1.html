Alplamayo Expedition

Description

The assent has a 70+ degree slope of snow and iced. Due to its technical nature, it requires previous experience and strong fitness. Furthermore, In the French System of technical grading it is rated AD+, but depending on conditions can also be D or D+.

One of our most popular expeditions pairs a climb of Alpamayo with an ascent of the neighboring Quitaraju. Directly to the east of Alpamayo, Quitaraju's summit is measured at 6,040m (19,820ft).

The mountain's north face has a 40 to 55 degree caked in crisp snow. In addition, the final push before Quitaraju's razor-sharp summit features a challenging but enjoyable period of ice climbing.
